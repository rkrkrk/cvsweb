computer generated images

High quality, realistic images can be created to accurately represent buildings, sites, interiors, new designs, engineering prototypes etc. Images can be created "off the plans", from drawings, sketches, a design brief or whatever is available. Colours, finishes, materials and textures can be evaluated. Images may be used for design review at the concept stage or for sales and promotion when going to market.

photomontages / composite images

Computer generated images and models can be merged with photographs to illustrate how a product or design will look in context. In visual assessment for property development, photomontages can show proposed buildings and sites in context with existing buildings and landscape.  Photomontages are often a requirement for planning applications.

3d floorplans and siteplans

3D representations of building interiors and 3D perspectives of site plans are a more effective means of illustrating size and layout.  Many people find it difficult to understand conventional 2D plans and drawings.

[examples...]

animation / virtual tour videos

Generated directly from architects plans and drawings, virtual tour videos incorporating animated flyovers of sites, walk-bys of exteriors and walk-throughs of interiors convey the layout and appearance of a development in a clear, easy to understand format.

Computer generated animation has many applications. Some of the projects we have worked on include: illustrating operating instructions and methods, assembly guides and instructions, demonstrating and analysing failure mechanisms, demonstrating production lines and factory layouts, reconstructing crashes and accidents.
